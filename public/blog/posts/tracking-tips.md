# 5 Tips for Better Weight Tracking

Consistent weight tracking is one of the most powerful tools for achieving your health goals. Here are five tips to make the most of your tracking journey.

## 1. Track at the Same Time Each Day

Your weight fluctuates throughout the day due to food, water, and other factors. For the most consistent readings:

- **Weigh yourself in the morning** after using the bathroom
- **Before eating or drinking** anything
- **Wear minimal clothing** or none at all

## 2. Don't Obsess Over Daily Changes

Your weight can fluctuate by 2-5 pounds daily due to:

- Water retention
- Sodium intake
- Carbohydrate consumption
- Hormonal cycles

**Focus on the trend** over weeks and months, not individual daily readings.

## 3. Use the Right Tools

GLPal makes tracking easy with:

- Quick-add weight entries
- Visual charts to see trends
- Multiple time period views (week, month, 3 months, year)
- Goal setting and progress tracking

## 4. Track Other Metrics

Weight is just one metric. Consider also tracking:

- Measurements (waist, chest, arms)
- Energy levels
- Sleep quality
- Medication adherence

This gives you a more complete picture of your progress.

## 5. Be Consistent

The most successful trackers:

- Log daily, even if traveling
- Review their progress weekly
- Adjust goals as needed
- Stay patient during plateaus

> Remember: Weight loss isn't linear. Stay consistent and trust the process.
