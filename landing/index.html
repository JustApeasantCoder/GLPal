Why I Built GLPal

I started using GLP-1 medications not too long ago. Like many people, I wanted a simple way to track my weight, log doses, and see my progress over time.

When I looked at existing apps, I noticed a pattern.

They wanted my Email.
They wanted my Data.
And eventually, they wanted my Money.

Some required accounts just to open the app. Others locked basic features behind subscriptions. Almost all of them collected more personal information than necessary.

I didn't want to trade my private health data just to use a simple tracker.

So I built one that doesn't ask for any of that.

GLPal Is Built Differently:

✓ No account required — open the app and start tracking immediately
✓ Your data stays on your device — stored locally in your browser
✓ No tracking or analytics — no third-party data collection
✓ No subscriptions — all features are free
✓ Your data remains yours — export anytime or back up to your own Google Drive
