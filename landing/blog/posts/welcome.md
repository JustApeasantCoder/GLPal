# Welcome to GLPal

We're thrilled to introduce GLPal, your new privacy-first companion for tracking GLP-1 medications, peptides, and weight progress.

## Why GLPal?

We built GLPal because we believe your health data should stay **your data**. Unlike other health apps that require accounts, cloud storage, and extensive permissions, GLPal keeps everything local on your device.

### Key Features

- **Weight Tracking** - Log your weight daily with easy-to-read charts
- **Medication Tracking** - Track GLP-1 doses and see your medication levels over time
- **Peptide Management** - Keep track of your peptide cycles and inventory
- **Privacy First** - All data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking

## Getting Started

1. Download GLPal from our website
2. Set your goal weight
3. Start logging your daily weight
4. Add your medications and peptides

## What's Next?

We're continuously working on new features. Here's what to expect:

- Cloud backup options (optional, encrypted)
- Data export in multiple formats
- More visualization options
- Integration with health devices

> Your health journey is personal. GLPal is here to support you without compromising your privacy.

Thank you for choosing GLPal. Here's to your health journey!
