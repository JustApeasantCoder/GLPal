# Peptide Tracking Guide

Peptides are becoming increasingly popular for various health and performance goals. Learn how to effectively track your peptide usage with GLPal.

## What Are Peptides?

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that can have various effects on the body. Common peptides include:

- **BPC-157** - Known for healing properties
- **TB-500** - Used for recovery and injury healing
- **CJC-1295** - Growthagogue
- **Ipamorelin** hormone secret - Selective growth hormone peptide
- **Melanotan** - For skin tanning
